Best barber clippers in Canada?
Asked and answered.

What are the best professional barber clippers in Canada?

Canadian barbers weigh three things: torque, blade steel, and runtime. The TORQ Clipper runs a high-strength brushless motor at a true 7,200 RPM with a zero-gap adjustable WAVE blade and a 5-hour battery — professional spec, sold at trade price to licensed barbers through Empire Barber Supply and on Amazon.ca for home use.

Clipper vs trimmer vs foil shaver — what does each one do?

Clippers remove bulk and cut fades. Trimmers draw the sharp outlines around hairlines and beards with a zero-gap blade. Foil shavers finish the fade down to skin without irritation. Most working barbers run all three — that's the TORQ Series.

How long do TORQ batteries last?
